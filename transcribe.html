Please Label the following tweets. Label the tweets as pro-gun regulation or anti-gun regulation. Then, please indicate if the tweet expresses "self-defense," a "fear of gun regulation," or both. These are defined below.

Pro-Gun Regulation: A tweet is a Pro-Gun Regulation tweet if the person tweeting supports gun regulation as judged from their tweet.

Anti-Gun Regulation: A tweet is an Anti-Gun Regulation tweet if the person tweeting is likely against gun regulation as judged from their tweet.

Self-Defense A tweet is considered "self-defense" if the person tweeting is likely to believe in a dangerous world. A belief in a dangerous world is the belief that people need self-defense weapons due to the world's inherent danger. The person tweeting may express the need to protect themselves against the dangers of the world.
